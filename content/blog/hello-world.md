Welcome to my dev blog — living inside a terminal, naturally.

This is the first post. I built this portfolio as a terminal emulator because I spend most of my time in one anyway. Why not make the portfolio feel like home?

## What this blog is about

- Things I'm building
- Bugs that wasted my Friday
- Opinions on tooling
- Occasional Rust rants

## Stack

The blog itself is just Markdown files fetched at runtime. No build step, no bundler, no framework. Just `fetch()` and `marked.js`.
